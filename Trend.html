Elden Ring
Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Released on February 25, 2022, for multiple platforms, the game is a collaborative project between Hidetaka Miyazaki, creator of the Dark Souls series, and George R.R. Martin, author of A Song of Ice and Fire. Elden Ring is set in a vast and richly detailed open world called the Lands Between, offering players an epic journey of exploration, combat, and storytelling.

The Witcher
The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an action role-playing game developed and published by CD Projekt Red. Released in May 2015, it is the third installment in The Witcher series, based on the book series by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski. Renowned for its richly crafted world, deep storytelling, and engaging gameplay, The Witcher 3 has received critical acclaim and numerous awards, including Game of the Year.

Minecraft
Minecraft is a popular sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios, initially released in 2011. Known for its pixelated block-based graphics and open-ended gameplay, Minecraft allows players to explore, build, and create in a procedurally generated 3D world.

Ghost Of Tsushima
Ghost of Tsushima is an action-adventure game developed by Sucker Punch Productions and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Released in July 2020 for the PlayStation 4 and later for PlayStation 5, the game is set in 1274 on the Japanese island of Tsushima during the first Mongol invasion of Japan. It blends historical settings with fictional storytelling to deliver a visually stunning and emotionally impactful gaming experience.
