Destiny 2
Destiny 2 is a popular free-to-play online multiplayer first-person shooter developed by Bungie. Released in September 2017, the game serves as the sequel to the original Destiny and offers a rich blend of storytelling, cooperative gameplay, and competitive experiences in a persistent online world. Players take on the role of Guardians—elite warriors empowered by the Light of the Traveler—to protect humanity from various alien threats and cosmic forces.

Modern Combact 5
Modern Combat 5: Blackout is a high-octane first-person shooter developed by Gameloft, released in 2014. The game is part of the critically acclaimed Modern Combat series and is available on multiple platforms, including iOS, Android, Windows, and Nintendo Switch. It offers an immersive military-themed experience with stunning visuals, engaging gameplay, and a variety of multiplayer and single-player modes.

Diablo
Diablo III is an action role-playing game (ARPG) developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. Originally released in May 2012 for PC and later expanded to consoles, it is the third installment in the Diablo series. The game combines dark fantasy storytelling with fast-paced, hack-and-slash gameplay, offering a rich, immersive experience for players.

Valorant
Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed and published by Riot Games. Released in June 2020, it combines precise gunplay with unique character abilities, creating a competitive and strategic gaming experience. The game draws inspiration from classics like Counter-Strike but introduces a fresh twist with its roster of Agents—characters with distinct powers and playstyles. Designed with esports in mind, Valorant has quickly become a global phenomenon in the competitive gaming community.
